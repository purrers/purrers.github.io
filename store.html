unearthed from amidst the muck and mire comes a furious split live tape from two Naarm diy worldbuilders.
Recorded live, this release is a joint venture for shoegaze titans purrers and deconstructed post-punk duo klunt. it’s haunted and murky, akin to a grey day cracked momentarily by the sun’s tormented eye peering through the cover of cloud. A strange and elusive date, a terminal irony, a tortured ascension to climax.

recorded as part of a fundraiser show, all proceeds from these tapes will go towards Dhadjowa Foundation.
